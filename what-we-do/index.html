WHat we do



CRTA provides real-world engineering and technology experiences for students as well as fostering learning in communication, leadership, global citizenship, creative thinking and team building skills.



Students are given the opportunity to learn: CAD, welding, computer

programming, electronics, mechanics, and robotics while developing other

valuable skills such as problem solving, teamwork, project management, public speaking, media/marketing and fundraising. We ensure all students have access to high quality STEM experiences such as competing in the FIRST® (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) FLL, FTC and FRC Robotics programs.

FIRST® Lego® League


Students research a real-world engineering challenge, develop a solution,

design, build, program and compete with LEGO MINDSTORMS®-based robots.

Who: Students in grades 5 - 8

Dates: August 29 till the end of February.

Time Commitment: 2.5 hrs/week

FIRST® Tech Challenge


Teams are challenged to design and build a robot using a kit of parts and within a common set of rules to play a sophisticated field game against other teams’ creations.

Who: Students in grades 7 - 12

Dates: September 9 till the middle of February.
